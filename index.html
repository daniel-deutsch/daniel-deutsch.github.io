Studies

Effects of Social Media Bots on the Crypto Market

Scraped over 6M twits from the Stocktwits social media, built two neural networks: one to classify each one of the users as either human or bot and one to identify the sentiment behind each post. A signal based on the engagement rate, user type and sentiment of each post is then generated and its effects over crypto assets' price variations is studied.

Deep Learning Statistics Big Data NLP Sentiment Analysis Time Series BERT

How User Information Drives Amazon's Products Recommendations

Scraped information about more than 60,000 Amazon's products by mimicking the behaviour/interests of seven different personas (sports, shopping, science, home, health, games and computers) through the usage of cookieas and HTTP request parameters. Proceded, then, to analyse whether there is evidence of price discrimination or price steering between each persona's recommended products. Later on, analysed the importance of each seller's features on Amazon's seller recommendation on the product's buy-box.

Data Scraping Cookies Manipulation Statistics

Effects of the Interest Rate on the Brazilian Stock Market

The impacts of the interest rate on eight distinct sectors of the Brazilian stock market were examined using financial indices as proxies for each sector. The research was conducted by applying both a time series (SARIMAX) and a linear regression model.

Time Series SARIMAX Linear Regression

Analysis of Restaurant Competition in Paris

Scraped information about more than 250,000 reviews from over 13,000 parisian restaurants on the Yelp social platform and proceeded to analyse the effects of restaurant competitiveness on review fraud, both for self improvement and for harming competitors. Later, the polarization difference between fake and genuine reviews, as well as the characteristics of users who publish fraudulent reviews, were examined.
